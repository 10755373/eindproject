Information

A warm welcome to the visualization page! Here you'll have the opportunity to have a look at them as well as numerous dropdowns and a hover to choose from. By doing this, you're able to see the numbers you're interested in as well as seeing what differences arise when chosing for another value.

The first two visualizations are meant to compare countries with eachother. The first option you can use is a dropdown which provides you with the posibility to chosse a gender and a certain age-group. After doing this, the map and scatterplot will show you the numbers for the year 1987 (since this year is set as default).
